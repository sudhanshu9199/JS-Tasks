How to Play:

  • A secret number (1-10) is hidden behind one of the doors
  • Enter your guess in the input field and click Submit
  • You have 3 attempts to find the correct number
  • After each guess, you'll get feedback on your attempt
  • When the game ends, all doors will be revealed
  • Click "Reset Game" to play again
